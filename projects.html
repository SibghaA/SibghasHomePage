Last Bite Standing

An app that makes group decision making easier and gamifies it.

LastBite is a social decision-making app that solves the common problem of group dining indecision. The app gamifies restaurant selection by allowing each participant to input their preferred cuisine type and then takes turns letting players veto options they don't want. Using Google Maps and Places API integration, LastBite automatically fetches nearby restaurants based on the group's cuisine preferences, along with ratings and location data. Players take turns viewing restaurant cards and can swipe left to veto options they dislike. The app also adds a "special pick" - a highly-rated restaurant of a different cuisine type - to broaden dining horizons. Once all vetos are used, the final remaining restaurant is selected, and users can immediately open it in Google Maps for directions. This interactive approach makes group restaurant selection fun while ensuring everyone has a say in the final decision.

Technologies used: Android SDK, Java Development Kit (JDK), Gradle, Google APIs Integration, Location & Permission Handling, Android Support Libraries, Data Management, JUnit.
