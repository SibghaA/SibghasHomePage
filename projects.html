Bias Mitigation in ML Algorithms for Chronic Disease Detection

A comparative analysis of IBM's AIF360 toolkit for reducing bias in healthcare ML models.

This research project explores the critical intersection of machine learning and healthcare equity, focusing on bias mitigation in chronic disease detection algorithms. Using IBM's AI Fairness 360 (AIF360) toolkit, we developed and analyzed various ML models for heart disease prediction, implementing comprehensive bias detection and mitigation strategies. The study achieved significant results in reducing gender-based disparities while maintaining high model performance, with the Multilayer Perceptron (MLP) emerging as the best-performing model with a recall of 0.822 and an F1 score of 0.778.

Technologies and Methods Used: Python, IBM's AIF360 Toolkit, Scikit-learn, Neural Networks, Support Vector Machines, Gradient Boosting, Statistical Analysis, Data Preprocessing, Bias Detection and Mitigation Techniques.

Last Bite Standing

An app that makes group decision making easier and gamifies it.

LastBite is a social decision-making app that solves the common problem of group dining indecision. The app gamifies restaurant selection by allowing each participant to input their preferred cuisine type and then takes turns letting players veto options they don't want.

Technologies used: Android SDK, Java Development Kit (JDK), Gradle, Google APIs Integration, Location and Permission Handling, Android Support Libraries, Data Management, JUnit.
