Silicon Prep

(Visit the website)

We empower high school students of all backgrounds by teaching them to excel in software engineering. Our self-developed course teaches the coding used by professionals in industry today.

After spending some time learning html, CSS and JavaScript, we decided to build our own basic course and teach in-person and online classes with our primary focus being on underprivilaged schools. Together with our volunteer team - who have worked at companies including Google and S&P Global - we ensure our students in Pakistan become the next generation of outstanding engineers.
